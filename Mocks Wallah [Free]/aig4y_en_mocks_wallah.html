  • From 19 July 2011 to 15 June 2012 → Nearly 1 year


Leap Years [Extra Day in February]
Leap years: 2004, 2008, 2012 → 3 leap years
Normal years: 2001, 2002, 2003, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2009, 2010, 2011 → 9 normal years
Count Days from 19 July 2011 to 15 June 2012

  • January 2012 → 31 days

  • February 2012 [leap year] → 29 days

  • March 2012 → 31 days

  • April 2012 → 30 days

  • May 2012 → 31 days
