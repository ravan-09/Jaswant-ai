The cat catches mice. Mice ________ by the cat.

are caught
were caught
have been caught
is caught
Solution

The correct answer is Option (1)

Key Points

  • Type of the sentence: Active voice.
  • Tense of the sentence: Present Simple Tense.
  • Subject: The cat. Object: Mice.
  • Format in Active Voice: Subject + V1/V(s/es) + Object.
  • Format in Passive Voice: Object (Subjective case) + is/are + V3 + by + Subject (Objective case).
  • "Mice" is the plural form of "mouse." Hence it will take the plural helping verb (are).
    • The cat catches mice. (Active)
    • Mice are caught by the cat. (Passive)

Therefore, the correct passive form of the sentence is "Mice are caught by the cat."
