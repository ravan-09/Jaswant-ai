50

Solution

Let the number of bulls be ‘a’ and the number of hens be ‘b’.

According to question:

4a + 2b = 2a + 2b + 48

4a + 2b – 2a – 2b = 48

2a = 48

a = 24

So, the number of bulls is 24.

Hence, ‘24’ is the correct answer.
